About Our Parastatal

Our parastatal is a government-owned organization based in Machakos Town, established with the primary objective of fostering socio-economic growth, community welfare, and sustainable development. We engage in various projects that enhance the well-being of our local communities, focusing on infrastructure, healthcare, education, and environmental protection.

Our Mission

Our mission is to contribute to the overall development of Machakos Town by delivering efficient, innovative, and sustainable services that enhance the quality of life for all residents. We aim to build a thriving, self-sufficient community while promoting economic stability and environmental sustainability.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading parastatal in Kenya, recognized for its outstanding contribution to community development and environmental sustainability. We envision a future where Machakos Town becomes a model of modern, well-planned urban growth that meets the needs of both current and future generations.

Our History

Founded in 2000, our parastatal has played a crucial role in the development of Machakos Town. Over the years, we have executed several successful projects aimed at improving infrastructure, public health, and local business development. Our history is marked by our commitment to creating lasting positive change in the community, ensuring that each initiative is aligned with the overall goals of sustainable development.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of services that address the diverse needs of the Machakos community. These services include but are not limited to:

  • Infrastructure Development and Maintenance
  • Environmental Conservation Initiatives
  • Public Health and Safety Programs
  • Social Welfare and Empowerment Projects
  • Local Business Support and Partnerships
  • Education and Capacity Building Programs
  • Water Supply and Sanitation Services

Our Objectives

  • Promote sustainable economic development through strategic planning and execution of projects.
  • Improve the living standards of the community by enhancing public services, including healthcare and education.
  • Encourage environmental sustainability by implementing eco-friendly initiatives and conservation programs.
  • Foster partnerships with local businesses to drive economic growth and create job opportunities.
  • Provide infrastructure that supports urban development while maintaining a balance with nature.
  • Ensure efficient use of resources to maximize the impact of every project undertaken.
